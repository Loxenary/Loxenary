## üí´ About Me

I am a developer who approaches engineering with a **business-oriented mindset**, focusing on building systems that deliver clear value rather than code for its own sake.

That perspective was not my starting point. I began as a software engineer focused on writing the best code possible‚Äîclean, scalable, and technically sound. Quality was measured primarily by correctness and structure.

**Game development expanded that foundation.** Working with complex, evolving codebases sharpened my understanding of system architecture, iteration costs, and long-term maintainability under real constraints. I've led teams in national competitions (Gemastik, Gameseed) and built a fully functional action-roguelike in just 48 hours at the Garena Game Jam.

Over time, I realized that strong engineering alone is insufficient. Code that does not serve a product goal, user need, or production reality ultimately creates cost, not value. Since then, my decisions have shifted toward **prioritization, trade-offs, and impact**‚Äîbalancing technical quality with time, resources, and outcomes.

**Today, I build with intent:** engineering choices are evaluated by the value they unlock.
